About Me

A recent graduate from NUS with a degree in Material Science & Engineering. Graduating during the peak of the COVID 19 pandemic, I knew I had to pivot. I took up programming and eventually fell in love with it. The idea of being able to create something so quickly (In comparison to Material Science) excites me. Furthermore, I thoroughly enjoy the never ending learning aspect of software engineering and the power to bring systems together to solve actual problems.

That being said, my time in university didn't go to waste. In university, I have spent the majority of time developing the primary skill needed for a Material Scientist, Innovation, through spending over 2 years in research and development including a 1 year stint with A*Star as well as achieving a 2nd runner up position in a design project where we pitched our ideas to industry experts such as 3m.

Looking back, I have no regrets venturing into this field and I'm confident this is something I will do for a very long time.
